Alex Stuart

Student. Photographer. Developer.

Who?

Hero. Rockstar. Genius.

Unfortunately, none of these words describe Alex Stuart.

But there is still a lot he has on offer. Alex is a Computer Science student at Aberystywyth University who grew up in North West England and currently lives and works in South Germany. By day, he works with a variety of programming and Mark Up languages, but by night he becomes a travelling Photographer with a passion for singing and Scuba Diving, who'll do just about anything for Mexican food.

Sound interesting? Look below to find out more!

What's on Offer?

Programmer

With experience in programming Object Orientated Programming languages like Java and C# , working with XSL and markup writing and formatting, honed Management and Documentation techniques, and finally just a light sprinkling of design flare, you've got the ingredients well rounded team member raring to go on any project you throw at him.

Web Developer

We all know how important the web is right now, and how important it is to be on it. Alex is ready for this, with practical training in technologies ranging from HTML5 and CSS, to PHP and SQL. Coding isn't the only thing to worry about with site development, but background research in SEO, frameworks and emerging technolgies means you can trust Alex with your site.

 

 

Professional

From Multi-billion Dollar international Companies like Areva and NuStar to private freelancing and charitable positions, Alex has a varied experience of working and managing projects in real world environments. From Musical Show Publicity Manager to IT team member to Freelancing Photographer, all it takes is the right offer to begin seeing professional and attractive results.

Life Liver

You can live to work, or you can work to live. Alex is a firm believer in the second camp, and tries to spend his spare time travelling and exploring by bike, foot or whatever transport is available. When he can, nothing beats donning a wet suit and scuba diving. His greatest love, Photography, joins him on all his adventures to document each chapter and chance he turns to.
